Yamaha Sniper 155R ABS: A Game-Changer in Underbone Racing

By Jc Cabico

The Yamaha Sniper 155R ABS is more than just a stylish underbone bike — it's a machine built for performance, precision, and street dominance. Designed with inspiration from Yamaha's racing DNA, this model brings a sporty aesthetic with a host of upgraded features that make it stand out from its predecessors.

One of the biggest highlights of the Sniper 155R ABS version is its Anti-lock Braking System (ABS), which greatly enhances rider safety by preventing wheel lock-up during sudden braking. Powered by a 155cc liquid-cooled, 4-valve engine with Variable Valve Actuation (VVA), the bike offers impressive power and fuel efficiency — ideal for both urban commuting and weekend rides.

Its modern LCD instrument panel, LED lighting system, assist and slipper clutch, and aggressive bodywork elevate the rider experience. Meanwhile, the ergonomics of the bike ensure comfort without compromising control, whether you’re navigating city traffic or taking on winding roads.

For Filipino riders and fans of sporty underbone motorcycles, the Sniper 155R ABS sets a new benchmark. With a blend of racing technology and real-world usability, this bike proves Yamaha’s commitment to innovation and rider satisfaction.
